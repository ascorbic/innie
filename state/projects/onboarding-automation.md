# Onboarding Automation

## Problem

New engineers joining the team spend their first week:
- Chasing access requests through multiple systems
- Finding out-of-date documentation
- Not knowing who to ask about what

The process is scattered across Notion, Slack threads, and tribal knowledge.

## Desired Outcome

A streamlined onboarding experience where:
1. New hires have a clear checklist that tracks completion
2. Access requests are batched and tracked
3. Key people/teams are introduced proactively
4. First-week goals are clear and achievable
